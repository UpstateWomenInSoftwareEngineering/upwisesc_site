Welcome to UpWiSE

Upstate Women in Software Engineering

Powered by

Join One of Our Meetup Sessions

We are an empowered group within RefactorGVL, serving the upstate of South Carolina.

Through the support of RefactorGVL, we are able to assist women across South Carolina in building their technical skills and leadership abilities.

We support women who are interested in entering technology roles.

We provide training sessions, resources, projects, and career advice to help women on their journey.

We help women who are already in technology fields persist and excel.

We have many members who are mid to late career who share their wisdom with us.

We create opportunities for learning, curiosity, and connection.

We foster an inclusive and positive environment throughout our meetups, projects, and events.
